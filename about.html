Who We Are

The All Nepal National Free Students Union (ANNFSU) is a progressive student organization dedicated to fostering leadership, enhancing academic standards, and addressing student concerns across the country. Established with the vision of empowering youth and promoting democracy in education, ANNFSU has played a significant role in shaping the academic and social landscape of Nepal.

At the Rautahat District Committee, we focus on localized efforts to connect students with opportunities for growth and advocacy. From organizing educational campaigns to supporting student welfare programs, our committee is at the forefront of creating positive change in the district.

With a dedicated team of passionate individuals, we aim to provide a platform for every student to voice their opinions, explore their potential, and make a lasting impact. Together, we are building a stronger, more inclusive student community for the future.
